Meet Our General Overseer

REV DR MERCY EZEKIEL

Rev. Dr. (Mrs.) Mercy O. Ezekiel is the current General Overseer of Christian Pentecostal Mission International. She was the pioneer National/International Coordinator of Christian Pentecostal Mission International with all the over one thousand branches in Nigeria and overseas under her administrative office.

She is the President of Pentecostal International Bible Seminary Lagos: and Founder/President of MEEZ Outreach that comprises Faith Clinic (a non-denominational outreach where people’s spiritual and physical needs are met by the Lord), Business Commission (an outreach for business men and women), Meez Empowerment Initiative, Pre-Natal Clinic for expectant mothers and others looking unto God for fruit of the womb, and Mercy Line (a charitable arm of her ministry where needs of the needy are met).

She also wrote a number of books – Power In the Word, Heart of A Champion, Prophetic Utterances – to mention a few.

She is an evangelist and pastor by calling, with proven and vibrant ministry evidenced by trails of miracles, signs and wonders.

Born in 1950, she attended Badagry Grammar School Lagos for her secondary education, and University of Lagos where she graduated with Bachelor’s Degree in Music. She also bagged Bachelor of Arts in Theology, Master of Arts in Theology, and Doctor of Philosophy in Theology. Having not done in her academic pursuit, she went further and obtained another Master of Arts – this time in Christian Education – from Christian College Texas, Diploma in Pastoral Education and Chaplaincy from The International Institute of Pastoral Certificates, and a certificate from Lagos Business School in Managing People for Strategic Advantage. She also bagged World Evangelism Certificate from Morris Cerullo Mission To All The World, Christian Lady Counselor and Pastoral Cadet, Christian Pastoral Counselor and Inspector.

She was married to Rev. Dr. O. Ezekiel (of blessed memory), the Founder/Pioneer General Overseer of Christian Pentecostal Mission International, and blessed with children.
